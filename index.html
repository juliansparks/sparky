WHAT IS THIS?

"The Adventures of Julian Sparks" is an animation production project revolving around a story full of magic, friendship, and the art of storytelling. This is a story meant to inspire others to make their own path and see their fantasies come to life.
The characters have diverse personalities and backgrounds. There is a character everyone can relate to. They are the driving force of this adventure.
Speaking of adventure, every episode is full of them. Each one has a lesson to teach. Each one is full of friendship and family. Each one is full of emotion. It's the bonds between the characters that get them through the tough times and their everyday lives.

...And now, it's time for those adventures to be told...

THE STORY

Everyone knows how fairytales usually go. The hero wins and the villain falls. But this isn't the fall of a villain. This is a story of his rising and that is where our titular character, Julian Sparks comes in.
On the night Julian was born, a king- an evil king- destroyed his entire kingdom, including himself, and sent his son, Lysandre, to the land of a group of magical beings called the Artists. The Artists were created by the stars to carry on the duties of creating the stories we all know and love, and to inspire the world. They were also granted the power to breathe life into anything they created. On that same night, the stars chose our hero- Err... villain as the new Artist.
Upon meeting Lysandre, Julian enters his role as the new Evil King and the Artist, as he explores how these two destinies can go hand-in-hand. As the Evil King, Julian lives in the shadow of Bryce White's story. His goal is to destroy Bryce before his timely "unhappily ever after" to escape the trap this story has set for him,
With his Artist powers, Julian created stories full of heroes, villains, and adventures. Little did he know that these stories would become very real and the characters would gain the ability to cross over to the real world through Julian's sketchbook. Since then, they took it upon themselves to explore what this new land has in store for them.
Now Julian will do whatever it takes to write his own story and get his happy ending once and for all.

THE WORLD

This world is not unlike ours. People wake up. They go to work/school. They have dinner and have fun. The only thing is that they have to deal with the fact that fairytales and magic aren't just figments of fantasy. This is a world where the gods walked above the humans, where fairies flutter their wings in the darkest of forests, and where evil kings or queens could rise to power.
Despite this, the magical side of this world is often hidden away. It only took one spark (or a Sparks) to inspire the magical beings to come out from the shadows.
Our story takes place in the sunny and buzzling city of Miami. Like the weather, things are about to get unpredictable in this city as some of our favorite fairytale villains are turning the pages on their stories. As these characters rise and fall, so do new lands. So where can Julian Sparks catch a break? Perhaps, in a castle off the dock by the beach. And something says this new kingdom will only expand in the next chapter.

Follow

Julian Sparks

The Evil King/The Artist

His sass, confidence, and determination are what makes this monarch unstoppable. It also helps that he has the magical ability to create anything he wants. Julian will go as far as it takes to get what he wants. He may seem distant, but he cares. Coming from a past full of ostracization, he holds a resentment towards most humans and is willing to take a stand to be his true self. Julian is often torn between his role in a fairytale story and his duty to the world, constantly trying to pull together shards from two different mirrors.
Follow

Jake Waterstone

The Hero

He is Julian’s best friend and greatest creation. In his world, he is one of the most significant heroes ever born and the prince of his home kingdom. Jake strives to get Julian on the right path and to give up his villainous ways. He is ready to serve as a hero in Julian’s world when necessary, even saving the world from Julian’s antics sometimes. Jake will go at lengths to see the good in everyone and find the light in the darkest of beings. Some may say that his story was the one that initiated a new era of heroes…
Follow

Braeden Octavius

The Dark Fairy

He is Miami’s newest villain and Julian’s new roommate. Known for mastering sleeping curses, Braeden has a few tricks up his sleeve. Braeden runs by his own rules and treats the world as his playground. Rumor has it that he cursed an entire kingdom to sleep. Despite his malicious ways, he can be a good friend to have when you need a push. Braeden tends to rival Jake in their friendships with Julian. Due to his past experiences with humans, he isn’t hesitant to help get Julian closer to his happy ending, or to take it for himself.
Follow

Bryce White

Snow White

Being the hero of his story, Bryce always took comfort in his impending happy ending. He's the president of his fraternity and one of the most popular figures in the city. He interns at his father's news media company, The Astral Times, and tries his hand at writing stories, like his nemesis, Julian Sparks. Bryce may let his high status and popularity feed him into an inflated ego, but he has the best intentions for everyone, including Julian. Despite their distance and differences, Bryce will always look out for the young Artist.
